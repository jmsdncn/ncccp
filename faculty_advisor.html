The coolest teacher on campus, Dr. Kehoe

Research

Dr. William Kehoe '95 graduated from University of California at Los Angeles (UCLA) with a bachelor of arts in biology, University of California at San Francisco (UCSF) with a Doctor of Pharmacy, and University of the Pacific with a masters in psychology with an emphasis in behavioral medicine. Dr. Kehoe completed a residency in hospital and clinical pharmacy at UCSF. He currently teaches in the areas of neurological and psychiatric pharmacotherapy, behavioral medicine and health psychology.

Dr. Kehoe is a clinical pharmacist at St. Joseph's Behavioral Health Center and at Green Brothers Pharmacy both in Stockton. He also serves as a consultant in clinical psychopharmacology for the developmentally disabled at Valley Mountain Regional Center, Stockton, CA. Dr. Kehoe is a Board Certified Pharmacotherapy Specialist by the Board of Pharmaceutical Specialties, and is certified in Applied Pharmacology by the American Board of Clinical Pharmacology.
