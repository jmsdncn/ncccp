Events to Help People

Kaiser Pharmacy Panel

3/21/13

NCCCP randomly selected 17 students to attend a networking and information panel event at Kaiser Permanente in Martinez, CA. As a committee our goal is to expand the knowledge and spark the interest of intern pharmacists at our school, specifically within the field of Clinical pharmacy.

CAC Carnival

4/27/13

We educated 5th grade students about the amount of sugar in a 2 liter of soda pop and had them participate in a hand-on activity to guess the amount of sugar. They were also able to guess how much fat was in a small bag of specific potato ships.

NCPA Woman's Health Fair

5/11/13

Northern California College of Clinical Pharmacy (NCCCP) Student Division hosts Stroke Prevention to educate the community about lifestyle changes, risk factors, stroke facts, ways to recognize stroke and important actions to take during when someone experiences a stroke.
